Chapter X

Lull’d in the countless chambers of the brain,  
Our thoughts are link’d by many a hidden chain:  
Awake but one, and lo! what myriads rise!  
Each stamps its image as the other flies!  
                    PLEASURES OF MEMORY

Emily pursued her journey, without any accident, along the plains of Languedoc towards the north-west; and, on this her return to Thoulouse, which she had last left with Madame Montoni, she thought much on the melancholy fate of her aunt, who, but for her own imprudence, might now have been living in happiness there!
