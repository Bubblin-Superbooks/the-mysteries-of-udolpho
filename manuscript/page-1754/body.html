You shall have none else.”

“Hah! who the devil have you brought home?” said they in bad Spanish, on perceiving the Count’s party, “are they from France, or Spain?—where did you meet with them?”

“They met with us, and a merry meeting too,” replied his companion aloud in good French. “This chevalier, and his party, had lost their way, and asked a night’s lodging in the fort.” The others made no reply, but threw down a kind of knapsack, and drew forth several brace of birds.
