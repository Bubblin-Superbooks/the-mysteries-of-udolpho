Chapter VIII

Be thou a spirit of health, or goblin damn’d,  
Bring with thee airs from heaven, or blasts from hell,  
Be thy intents wicked, or charitable,  

I will speak to thee.  
                    HAMLET

Count de Villefort, at length, received a letter from the advocate at Avignon, encouraging Emily to assert her claim to the estates of the late Madame Montoni; and, about the same time, a messenger arrived from Monsieur Quesnel with intelligence, that made an appeal to the law on this subject unnecessary, since it appeared, that the only person, who could have opposed her claim, was now no more.
